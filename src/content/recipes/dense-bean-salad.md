---
title: Dense Bean Salad
description: A fresh, hearty bean salad perfect for meal prep and serving with lettuce boats.
prepTime: 30
cookTime: 0
servings: 15
tags: [salad, meal-prep, mediterranean]

ingredients:
  - Cannellini beans
  - Pinto beans
  - Garbanzo beans
  - Great northern beans
  - Cilantro
  - 1 to 2 bell peppers
  - Tomatoes

dressing:
  - Honey (optional)
  - 3/4 packet taco seasoning
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - Garlic vinaigrette

instructions:
  - Drain and rinse the beans
  - Chop the cilantro and vegetables
  - Add everything to a large bowl
  - Add dressing ingredients
  - Toss until well combined
  - Taste and adjust
  - Serve with lettuce boats
---
